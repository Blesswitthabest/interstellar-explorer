# A Garden in Vacuum

The station looked wrong against the stars—too straight, too quiet.
No transponder, no reply, just a slow spin like it forgot it was abandoned.

{% statusCard "Derelict habitat cylinder" "O2: okay (for now)" "Moving slow, recording everything" %}

## Inside

Dust floated in my light like underwater sand.
I passed old signs and open cabinets and a hallway that felt like it was holding its breath.

## The greenhouse

A sealed module still had power.
When I opened it, I smelled something I haven’t smelled in too long: **plants**.

Not thriving. Not perfect. Just alive.

I took one small cutting back to my ship.
Not because it fixes anything… but because it proves something out here refused to quit.
